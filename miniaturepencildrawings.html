MINIATURE SERIES

I started the miniature series for two reasons:

1. A miniature drawing shouldn't take more than 30 minutes, which means, it is a great way of forcing myself to draw everyday with no excuses, as 30 minutes can certainly be manageble within 24 hours.
2. I have great difficult doing small drawings. Drawing in small spaces makes you loose a bit of detail richness, as the pores of the paper and the tip of the pencil remain the same size, but the features are now much smaller. It makes you upgrade your dexterity skills.

These miniatures were the fun way I found to manage both of these issues while studying portraits and the human form.

Their height are around 4cm and 4,5cm (around 1.65 inches).
