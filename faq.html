DRAWING

1. HOW DID YOU LEARN HOW TO DRAW?

I set the personal challenge of drawing the human face and used all types of  free knowledge sources available on the internet such as free books, Youtube videos about various mediums (pencils, pens, oil, pastels and digital) and communicating with other artists making questions about their techniques on their Instagram or Facebook pages. But most importantly: persistance. Drawing 40 minutes per day was also part of the challenge.

My free e-book "Pencil Artist Starter Pack" is my way of giving back to the community, who has been so generous with the free knowledge.

2. WHAT ARE YOUR MATERIALS?

My graphite pencils range from HB to 8B, an eraser, an eraser pencil, a blender and a hard-bristles brush, that's all. You don't need to get fancy to draw. I use several brands for pencils but I don't have a favorite one. Brand X can have very hard pencils, but hard pencils are great for textures so there's no such thing as a "bad brand". You can get some glimpses of my materials on my Instagram account.

DRAWING SERVICES

3. I'D LIKE TO MAKE MY HAIR LONGER, MY NOSE SMALLER AND CHANGE MY LIPS FOR ANGELINA JOLIE ONES. CAN YOU DO IT?

Yes, I can, but I won't. The only changes I will do upon request that differ the final drawing from the reference picture are erasing very datailed items such as visible pores or freckles on the skin, cropping a picture on the desired subject and not adding the background of the original picture, as a commission without these features will be cheaper.

Otherwise, I'll follow STRICTLY what your picture shows so I recommend that you choose a picture taken by a professional photographer to be used as reference. Some photographers do physiognomy change services and you can find Photoshop freelance professionals online so this can be sorted before the commission is set.

4. DO YOU ACCEPT COMMISSIONS BIGGER THAN A3?

Yes. For drawings smaller than A3, the price will be exactly the same you get from the pricing pages. For drawings bigger than A3, contact me for more information.

5. DO YOU FRAME YOUR DRAWINGS?

No. The product you will receive is  a drawing on a sheet of paper along with the package details you can see below. And because the paper isn't water proof, can get dirty and may fold depending on the way it is handled after you open your package, I recommend that you have a way of framing it settled before the commission arrives.

6. WHAT ARE "PROGRESS PICTURES"

Every business day after your commission is set, you'll receive via email pictures of the progress of the drawing so you know how it is taking shape, as it builds reliability and trust between the artist and the client. When the drawing is finished, the final pictures will be sent for approval so I know you're happy with the results before I send the finished drawing. 7. CAN I CANCEL MY ORDER?

You have 24 hours after purchase to cancel a commission. After these 24 hours, I'll start working on your drawing, and I don't cancel orders which I already started, neither give back the money.

This is not retail, but a highly personalized product made for each client.

SHIPPING

8. WHEN WILL MY COMMISSION ARRIVE?

As soon as your order ships, you will receive an e-mail with tracking and delivery information. Every type of commission has a specific number of business days to be finished and this can be checked at the Pricing page. For the shipping time, please add from 20 to 25 days for your purchase to arrive in your home country. If the drawing will be used to celebrate an important date or it'll be a wedding/birthday gift, please keep these informations in mind.

9. WHAT SHIPPING SERVICE DO YOU USE?

I use FedEx International Economy.

10. HOW WILL MY COMMISSION BE PACKAGED?

The drawing will have one sheet of paper on the front and another one on the back, they will be inside a clear plastic bag, this clear plastic bag will be between 2 sheets of styrofoam, while other 2 sheets of MDF boards will keep everything else from bending and transportation damage. The items will be held together with packing paper and duct tape.

11. CAN I SEND MY ORDER DIRECTLY TO A GIFT RECIPIENT?
Certainly - just inform me the address and gift recipient's name beforehand. You may also leave a printed gift message free of charge, send me the file and I'll print it in A5 size.

12. WHAT'S YOUR RETURN, DAMAGE AND LOST PACKAGE POLICY?
Because each drawing is custom made for you and it'll be shipped after the client's approval via email (so I know if you're happy with the results), I am unable to offer refunds after shipping. If the drawing is damaged during shipment or the package gets lost, I'll make another one free of charge but:

1. I will also be tracking the order via FedEx's website which means, I'll also know if the order was lost.

2. If the package was visibly damaged during shipment, take photos BEFORE you open it completely and photos of the damaged drawing, so I know it was faulty transportation.
