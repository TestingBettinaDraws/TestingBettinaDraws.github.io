I was born and raised in Taubat√©, Brazil. I don't have any formal education in arts and learned what I know (and still learning) by trial, error and hustle after setting the personal challenge of drawing the human face, as people who push themselves to limits outside their confort zone are people who inspire me. I began my professional journey as an artist after a difficult time in life, where I didn't have time to improvise and had to live with resources and knowledge I already had. Luckly, I had beat my personal challenge around that time :)

I like to think that I didn't choose art, but art chose me by making life take some odd twists.
My goal is to make traditional art easily accessible. Most people think that commissioning a piece of art  is something out of this world but I aim to bring beauty to your everyday life. Like Bill Gates wanted a personal computer in every single home, I want you and everyone else to a have a meaningful and personalized artwork on your walls.



My motivation to keep going on with the idea of transforming a personal challenge into a full-time job despite difficult times came from observing friends & family's reactions after I gifted them a drawing showing themselves.

So I decided to build this bridge between the artist and the muse. Make it easy for people in the whole world to have traditional artwork commissioned for themselves.
