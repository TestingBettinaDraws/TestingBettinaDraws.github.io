HOW DOES IT WORK?

To have a traditional artwork made specially for you has never been easier

1. Hover over PRICING

The PRICING pages will guide you through which type of commission is appropriate for your photo and your preferences.

2. Talk to the Bot.

After deciding your medium, click on CHECK FINAL PRICE to talk to the Bot. It'll guide you through the final commission price and purchase. If you're still unsure if the commission you selected is the best one for you, send me a message on the contact form below. 

2. An artwork being born

After the commission details are set and 50% of its value is paid, you'll receive pictures of the progress of the drawing every business day via email to see how your favorite wall art is taking shape! The F.A.Q. page has some examples of these pictures.

3. Done!

You'll receive pictures of the finished commission for final approval before the drawing is sent to its new home. Free shipping worldwide with tracking code for all orders.
