BALLPOINT PEN DRAWINGS

I wouldn't have improved my graphite pencil skills if it wasn't for the additional challenges a ballpoint pen brings to finishing an artwork: precision, dexterity and managing the fear of making mistakes: every stroke is a leap of faith.

Because there's no range of hardness like in graphite pencils, no smudging and no eraser, I noticed that the more I draw using ballpoint pens, the less I use the mentioned tools in a pencil drawing.
I carry the lessons of taking risks while keeping it simple everywhere.
